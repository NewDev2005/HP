How to make Chicken Nuggets?

In this recipe you will find out how to make some delicious Chicken Nuggets easily from the comfort of your home.

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken breasts
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
    • Dried basil
    • Dried thyme
    • Salt
  • Butter

How to make Chicken Nuggets in the oven?

  1. Cut the chicken breast into nuggets.
  2. Mix the bread crumbs, cheese and seasonings.
  3. Dip the nuggets in melted butter, then coat them in the bread crumb mixture.
  4. bake until the chicken is brown and cooked through.
